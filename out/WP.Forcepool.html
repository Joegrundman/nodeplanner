Class: Forcepool

Forcepool

WP.Forcepool class for creating forcepools. Forcepools are a popup dialog that contain units that have not been placed on the map or in a shipyard or taskforce these units are also put into categories (e.g. unbuilt, dow+1, air, naval, ground)
